Salmon Cookies History

Salmon is a common name for several species of ray-finned fish in the famility Salmonidae. Salmon is considered to be healthy due to it's high protein , high omega-3 and high vitamin A and D. Health benefits associated with omega-3 include relieving sympots of dry eye disease, improving cognitive function and helping to manage symptos of rheumatoid arthritis. Vitamin A helps with immune boasting and also improving reproduction and vision. Vitamin D is an immune-boosting nutrient and also helps our bodies to absorb calcium. Salmon also contains Vitamin B12 which is important in preventing weakness , tiredness, constipation and loss of appetite.Salmon is a great choice for the environmentally conscious eater, since eating salmon can do more than boost your own health. Alaska Salmon is both wild and sustainable, good for the environment and good for us.
